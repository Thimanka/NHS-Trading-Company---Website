About Our Company

Bridging the gap between global manufacturing and local demand.

Who We Are

NHS Trading Company was established with a singular focus: to provide the highest quality care label materials to the local garment manufacturing industry.

We specialize in importing premium jumbo rolls of care label fabrics directly from trusted, certified manufacturing partners in China. By purchasing in massive bulk, we bypass intermediaries, allowing us to offer exceptional quality at highly competitive prices to our local clients.

Our Supply Chain Process

Efficiency from the factory floor to your printing press.

1. Global Sourcing

We partner exclusively with top-tier textile mills in China that specialize in care label substrates, ensuring consistent coating and weave.

2. Bulk Import

Materials are imported via shipping containers in jumbo roll format, optimizing freight costs and reducing environmental impact per unit.

3. Local Distribution

From our central warehouses, we supply slitting companies, label printers, and garment manufacturers with exactly the stock they need.
